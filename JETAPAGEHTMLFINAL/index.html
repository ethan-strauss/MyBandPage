Welcome to the J.E.T.A band page!

We are a band from South West Michigan that began in the fall of 2020. We sought out to create music stemming from each member's unique outlook and influences in music, with our guitar player (Alex Torakis) being heavily influences by the metal and rock sound of the 70's and 80's, drummer (Tieler Shellington) being Lez Zepplin and Black Sabbbath, singer and rhythm guitarist (Johnny Posada) having a heavy Beatles influence, and bassist (Ethan Strauss) loving all music from the 90's.

We've brought all these influences together into a sound and experience we perform live that showcases everyone's strengths in the group. Watch our video below showcasing some of our songs from a benefit concert we played in September 2022.



We look forward to seeing everyone here at our future shows!
